RULES :-
CENTRAL HUB IS THE ORANGE COLOUR STRUCTURE
BASE STATION IS THE BLUE COLOUR STRUCTURE
COLLECT THE KEYS AND GO TO CENTRAL HUB TO CONVERT IT INTO THE SHARD
EVERY SHARD REQUIRES 5 KEYS
FROM TWO SHARD YOU CAN INCREASE THE SYSTEM HEALTH BY 5% AND PLAYER HEALTH BY 10%
YOU WIN IF SYSTEM HEALTH REACHES 100%
YOU WILL LOSE IF PLAYER HEALTH OR SYSTEM HEALTH BECOMES 0
SURVEILLANCE TOWER CAN BE DESTROYED BY BULLETS AFTER 3 HITS
BUILDINGS CAN BE DESTROYED BY BULLETS AFTER 2 HITS
CENTRAL HUB AND BASE STATION CANNOT BE DESTROYED
